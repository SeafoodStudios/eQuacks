Privacy Policy

Last updated: August 12, 2025

eQuacks only collects your username, and your password. You password is securely hashed using Argon2. Even though it uses a secure hashing mechanism, you should always keep your passwords strong and secure. Your information is stored in a JSON file and will never be sold. If you forget it, you cannot get your account back. Our hosting providers (Vercel and PythonAnywhere) log your IP address and other metadata to prevent user abuse and denial of service. By using this service, you agree to this.
